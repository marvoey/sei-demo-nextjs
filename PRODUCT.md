# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

RIAs (Registered Investment Advisors) and enterprise financial advisors evaluating a partnership with SEI. They're mid-career professionals making high-stakes business decisions about their practice's future. They arrive informed, skeptical of marketing fluff, and need to feel that SEI understands their world before they'll trust the relationship.

## Product Purpose

A marketing demo site that showcases SEI's integrated enterprise financial solutions and AI-driven personalization capabilities. Success looks like a financial advisor walking away convinced that SEI can support their transition — both operationally and relationally. The PartnerMatchWrapper is the centerpiece of that demo: it surfaces a simulated "we know you" moment, showing how SEI's platform matches advisors to the right partner.

## Brand Personality

Trusted · Sophisticated · Modern. The voice is that of a seasoned peer, not a sales pitch. Confident without being aggressive. Institutional without being stiff.

## Anti-references

- Old-guard finance: Fidelity/Vanguard-era dense text walls, navy + gold color stories, no spatial breathing room, no personality.
- Crypto/Web3: dark mode neon glows, token-first aesthetics, anything that reads speculative or volatile.

## Design Principles

1. **Peer, not vendor.** SEI speaks to advisors as equals who understand the business. Every word and interaction should feel like counsel, not a pitch.
2. **Confidence through restraint.** Sophistication is expressed by what's left out. Resist the urge to over-explain or over-decorate.
3. **Personalization earns trust.** The matched-partner experience only works if it feels genuinely tailored. Delight here is specific, not generic.
4. **Signal without noise.** Key moments (appointment confirmed, partner matched) should land cleanly. Don't let flourish compete with substance.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Respect prefers-reduced-motion for all animations. Adequate contrast on all text.
